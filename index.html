about

PSYCHARTIST is a photography studio with a difference.

We are four photographer-friends who have come together to create a new dimension in photography. Our creative skills in photography were honed in Light & Life Academy Ooty, under the mentorship of its renowned founders Mr Iqbal Mohammed and Mrs Anuradha. Psychartist is an offspring of much brainstorming, pow-wows and debates that have culminated in a single vision: creating a synthesis through our photography. Each of us has gained expertise in our specific discipline by assisting experienced photographers and has found his/her niche, be it editorial, commercial, corporate, food, fashion, industrial, as well as nature, travel, interiors and architecture. You name it and we visualize and click it.

We carry our cameras with compassion and curiosity to aim at perfection and create something extraordinary.
